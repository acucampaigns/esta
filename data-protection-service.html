Data protection statement

We collect and use your personal data exclusively within the scope of the data protection law of the Federal Republic of Germany. In the following, we would like to inform you of the type, scope and purposes of the collection and use of personal data. You can view this notification on our website at any time.

Data transmission and logging for system and statistical purposes
When you access our website, your Internet browser automatically sends data to our web server for technical reasons. This data includes the date and time of access, the URL of the referring website, the requested file, the quantity of data sent, browser type and version, operating system and your IP address. This data is stored separately from other data entered by you when using our site. It is therefore not possible for us to assign this data to any given person. This data is analysed for statistical purposes, after which it is deleted.

Customer master data
Insofar as a contractual relationship is to be established between you and us, or provisions of such an agreement are to be specified or changed, we will collect and use your personal data to the extent that this is required for these purposes.
